This piece explores the concept of "Digital Presence." Using Google's **XR Blocks** library, we instantiate a high-fidelity avatar—referencing the Gemini Icebreaker demos—surrounded by a floating gallery of digital artifacts.

Unlike the static Cube, this entity is aware of the spatial context, anchoring itself in your reality while curating a collection of floating memories.
